Methodology Summary

Data: Consolidated bilateral panel dataset (2015‚Äì2021) combining UNCTAD maritime trade flows, FAO Food Balance Sheet indicators, CEPII gravity variables, and liner shipping connectivity metrics.

Methods: Structural gravity models estimated via OLS and PPML with fixed effects, benchmarked against machine-learning-based forecasting using artificial neural networks (ANN).

Scope: Commodity- and corridor-level analysis of maritime agri-food trade with forward-looking baseline projections to support food security risk assessment.
